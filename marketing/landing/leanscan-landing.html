Frequently Asked

Questions, answered honestly.

Is this medical advice or a medical device?
No. LeanScan is a nutrition tracking tool. It doesn't replace your prescribing physician or registered dietitian. We help you log what you're already doing — more accurately and more easily.
How accurate is the AI photo recognition?
Honestly: it's directionally right, not lab-precise. The AI estimates protein and calories within roughly 10-20% — same as any photo-based tracker. On a GLP-1 medication where total intake is already low, that accuracy is more than enough to track trends and hit daily protein floors. You can always adjust manually.
When does the app launch?
iOS launches in early access for waitlist members within 8 weeks of reaching 1,000 signups. Android follows roughly 8 weeks after iOS. Founder pricing is locked in for waitlist members forever.
What about my data and privacy?
Your meal photos and health logs stay on your device by default. We never sell data. We never share with insurance companies, pharma, or advertisers. If you delete the app, your data is gone.
Will this work if I'm not on a GLP-1?
Yes — anyone trying to prioritize protein during weight loss will benefit. But the app is opinionated for GLP-1 users. If you want a generalist tracker with 200 features, MyFitnessPal already exists. LeanScan does one thing well.
Who's building this?
A small independent team of software engineers and nutrition researchers. We're not VC-funded, we're not pharma-backed. Just builders trying to make the tool we wished existed for people we know on these medications.
