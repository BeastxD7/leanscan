# sales/

Pricing, conversion materials, customer cohort tracking.

## Current pricing (from context doc § 7)

- **Founder cohort** (first 500 users): $49/year, locked in for life
- **Public launch:** $89/year or $9.99/month
- **Free trial:** 7 days, no card required
- **No monthly-only option until 1,000 paying users** (annual prepay = cash flow)

## What goes here over time

- `cohort-tracker.xlsx` — signup → trial → paid funnel by week
- `churn-analysis.md` — once you have customers
- `pricing-experiments.md` — A/B test results when you start running them
- `objection-handling.md` — patterns of why people don't sign up, drawn from email replies

## Not the same as marketing/

`marketing/` brings them to the door. `sales/` is what converts the visit into payment and keeps them paying. There's overlap (landing page CTA, pricing copy, founder cohort messaging) but the day-to-day work is different.
